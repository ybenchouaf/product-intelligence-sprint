# Workforce planning lifecycle — Bridgit context

Source: v0.1 seed, section 6. Maps where Bridgit capabilities attach to customer workflow.

## Lifecycle stages

### 1. Pipeline and project intake

Projects and pursuits enter from CRM, preconstruction, PM systems, or manual import.

**Bridgit role:** Integrations sync pursuit, project, and people data — reduce "CSV gymnastics."

### 2. Role demand definition

Teams define roles, dates, allocation, location, requirements.

**Bridgit role:** Project Planning — roles/assignments, bulk updates, drag-and-drop, AI import from screenshots/CSVs, alerts.

### 3. Supply visibility

Planners view people, availability, experience, time off, location, current/future assignments.

**Bridgit role:** Project Planning — time off, mobile, maps, project hubs.

### 4. Matching and recommendations

Match people to roles.

**Bridgit role:** Smart Suggestions — availability, experience, commute, role fit; AI explanations.

### 5. Forecasting and scenario planning

Compare future supply and demand.

**Bridgit role:** What-if pursuit scenarios, roles for hire, utilization, bench cost.

### 6. Staffing meeting and decision review

Recurring meetings remain common; Bridgit reduces prep burden.

**Evidence:** Suffolk quote — assigned people before meetings; reduced 20-person Excel-heavy meeting to shorter monthly process. **Confidence: Medium-high** (customer quote via Bridgit).

**Friction opportunity:** Meeting prep, decision logs, follow-through (seed section 14).

### 7. Communication and change management

**Bridgit role:** Communication module — scattered email/spreadsheet context, assignment alerts, broadcasts, notes.

### 8. Reporting and executive review

Executives review utilization, bench cost, pipeline, hiring needs, workforce health.

**Bridgit role:** Speed-to-decision, go/no-go scenarios, executive visibility (customer quotes in seed).

### 9. Historical learning / experience capture

**Bridgit role:** Experience Tracking and Internal Resumes — project history by assignment, build type, market sector, owner, custom fields.

## Workflow friction summary (cross-ref)

| Friction | Confidence |
|----------|------------|
| Spreadsheet dependence (71% per Bridgit-sponsored survey) | High |
| Meeting-heavy planning | High |
| Scattered communication | High |
| Messy unstructured inputs | High |
| HR/Ops handoff | High |
| Crew communication (specialty) | High |
| Data trust for AI | Medium-high |
