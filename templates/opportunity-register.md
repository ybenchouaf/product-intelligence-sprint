## How to use

- Assign stable IDs: `O-001`, `O-002`, …
- **Status**: `exploratory` (thin evidence), `grounded` (cited evidence + hypotheses), `ready` (prototype gate met), `parked`, `retired`
- Link **evidence** and **hypothesis** IDs — required before `grounded`
- **Test** describes the smallest experiment that would validate or invalidate the opportunity
