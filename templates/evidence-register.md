## How to use

- Assign stable IDs: `E-001`, `E-002`, …
- **Type** is `fact` (verifiable) or `observation` (noticed in use or materials)
- Every row needs a **source** and **artifact** link where possible
- Inferences and hypotheses do not belong here — register those separately
